# v3.0.0

That's it, folks.

After over a year of intense development, `v3.0.0` (aka: LIGA 1) is finally finished!

A massive thank you goes out to our growing [discord](https://discord.gg/ZaEwHfDD5N) community for providing such valuable feedback, feature requests, and even contributions that have helped shape the project into what it is today.

> [!NOTE]
> Special mention to Rxpev who has been doing an unbelievable job with his [LIGA: Pro Journey](https://github.com/rxpev/LIGA-Pro-Journey#-features) total conversion mod that focuses on player careers.

Below is a summary of some of the most notable features since [The Final Beta Build](https://github.com/orgs/playliga/discussions/569).

## Start Careers as an Existing Team

![existing](resources://markdown/whats-new/existing.png)

An oft-requested feature is the ability to start a new career as an existing team. Take the reigns of a struggling team in the `premier` division or guide a `main` team up into the top flight.

## Player Contracts

![contracts](resources://markdown/whats-new/contracts.png)

The transfer market is now more dynamic with player contracts. Once locked-in to a contract, their wages will come out of your earnings for the duration of the agreement. Miss payments and debt starts piling up.

Keep avoiding payments long enough and the players will walk.

Lastly, if you do not have enough players to play or sim your matches you will be forced to forfeit those games.

## Standalone CS:GO

![csgo](resources://markdown/whats-new/csgo.png)

The app now supports the standalone CSGO installation that can be installed alongside CS2.

- [Counter-Strike: Global Offensive on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4465480/CounterStrikeGlobal_Offensive/)

Please note that the CS:GO legacy branch within CS2 beta builds is no longer supported.

## Player XP Decay

![decay](resources://markdown/whats-new/decay.png)

In Counter-Strike, every millisecond matters, and the march of time hits your players hard. As they age, they gain XP more slowly and eventually their XP starts to decay.

## Discord Server

![discord](resources://markdown/whats-new/discord.png)

There is now a discord server!

The server aims to keep everyone informed about the latest developments and serve as a hub for streamlined feedback. You'll be able to share suggestions, report bugs, and stay up-to-date with the project, all in one place.

- [discord.gg/ZaEwHfDD5N](https://discord.gg/ZaEwHfDD5N)
